Who is Lindsey? I grew up and still live in the Phoenix area. After high school, I held a few odd jobs before getting hired by a local healthcare company. Since then, I've spent about 15 years learning the business office aspect of our hospitals and the healthcare industry, working my way up our department. I developed a good sense for improving and automating processes throughout the business office, and I enjoy looking at problems and finding solutions to improve our operation. In the time after high school I've also earned an Associate's Degree in Computer Information Systems.

When I'm not at work, one of my favorite hobbies is playing ice hockey. Most people think it's odd living in a desert, but I think it makes even more sense that way. When it's 115 degrees outside that ice rink is nice and cool. Everyone else is missing out!

In the past few years I've also started learning photography, particularly using a DSLR camera as opposed to a little point and shoot camera. A nice thing about Arizona, despite its reputation as a desert, you don't actually have to go far to get all sorts of different scenery- we have deserts, forests, mountains, snow, all of which makes for great photos. And it's not hard to head over to California if I want to try some ocean photographs.
